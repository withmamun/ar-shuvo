Social Media Marketing

Social media marketing (SMM) is a key digital marketing strategy that delivers unique benefits for brands. SMM is the process of companies using social media to advertise and promote their products or services while allowing them to connect in a more organic way with existing and potential customers.

Market Analysis

Social media managers are responsible for driving sales through the pipeline, building brand awareness and increasing webs traffic from platforms like Facebook, Instagram, Twitter, Pinterest and TikTok. They implement a brand's marketing strategy and ensure its messaging is delivered accurately on these channels.
